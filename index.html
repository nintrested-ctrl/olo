Simple tracking. Useful reports.

TimeTrack focuses on the data people actually understand: how much time they spent, where it went, and how it changes over time.

Daily screen time

See your total PC time for the day with a clear, enlarged dashboard number.

App usage

Track software and games with app names, icons, time totals, and visual bars.

Reports

View daily, weekly, and monthly reports with graphs and top-app summaries.

Categories

Break usage into Productivity, Entertainment, Creativity, and Other.

Idle detection

Control whether idle or away time counts, useful for videos or long sessions.

Cloud sync beta

Experimental backup and PC-to-PC sync using a private sync key.

Local-first by default.

TimeTrack stores usage data on your own Windows PC unless you choose to enable beta cloud sync. The goal is awareness, not surveillance.

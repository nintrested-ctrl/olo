Features

Built around the way people actually use PCs.

The point is not just “count time.” TimeTrack is built to explain where your time goes, separate real activity from away time, and give you enough control to correct categories when the app gets context wrong.

What TimeTrack helps answer

Where did my day go? Which apps took the most time? Was I actually active, or was the PC just left on? What part of my browser use was work, entertainment, or creative activity?

Features

What TimeTrack does.

TimeTrack gives you a clear view of your Windows PC usage without turning productivity into a complicated dashboard.

Daily screen time

See your total active PC time for the day in a clear dashboard. Useful when you want to know whether your time was controlled or wasted.

App usage tracking

Track how long you spend in each app, game, browser, or work tool. TimeTrack makes it obvious which apps are taking most of your day.

Reports

View daily, weekly, and monthly summaries with simple graphs, top-app breakdowns, and history so you can spot patterns over time.

Categories

Group your time into Productivity, Entertainment, Creativity, and Other. This makes your screen time easier to understand at a glance.

Idle detection

TimeTrack can detect when you are away from the keyboard and separate idle time from active usage, depending on your settings.

Browser breakdown

Browser usage can be broken down into common sites such as YouTube, ChatGPT, Gmail, Twitch, Netflix, GitHub, and more where supported.

Dark and light mode

Use a cleaner bright interface during the day or switch to dark mode when you want something easier on the eyes.

Cloud sync beta

Experimental PC-to-PC backup and sync lets you test syncing TimeTrack data between devices using a private sync key.
